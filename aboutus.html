About Us

Meet the team and our story behind Hot Beans

Our Story

Hot Beans began with a simple idea — to create a welcoming space where passionate coders and coffee enthusiasts could connect, create, and collaborate. Founded in 2022, we’ve grown from a cozy local café to a vibrant hub for the tech community, fueled by a love for both expertly brewed coffee and innovative code.

Our Mission

We strive to inspire creativity, foster learning, and build connections. At Hot Beans, we believe the best ideas are sparked over a great cup of coffee, surrounded by like-minded people who are always eager to share and grow together.

Our Values

  • Welcoming Community
  • Quality in Every Cup
  • Continuous Learning
  • Collaboration & Innovation
  • Supporting Local & Global Causes
